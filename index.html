Matcha Tea Ceremony

This workshop is a collective and immersive experience inspired by the traditional Japanese Matcha tea ceremony. I guide each step with intention, contemplation and mindful tasting of this traditional Japanese elixir while exploring its history and cultural significance.

Self-awareness Mindfulness

Sashiko Studio

This workshop explores Sashiko, a traditional Japanese stitching technique known for its geometric patterns. I teach participants through mindful textile experimentation using colorful patchwork pieces created with intention, focus and calm attention.

Patterns Tradition
🖋️

Flow Calligraphy

This workshop focuses on Chinese and Japanese calligraphy practices using watercolor ink techniques and lettering markers. I incorporate meditative phrases inspired by the Tao Te Ching and Japanese philosophies such as Ikigai, Kintsugi and Wabi-sabi. The intention is slow practice, mindful strokes and emotional reconnection through movement and expression.

Tao Te Ching Flowing with the natural rhythm of life
📓

Emotional Journaling

This therapeutic writing practice combines journaling, collage and drawing to explore thoughts, emotions and personal experiences through physical or digital formats. I use these creative techniques to encourage self-awareness, reduce stress and help organize ideas intentionally while allowing participants to reinterpret life experiences with compassion and meaning.

Creativity Meditation in motion
